Ireland says YES!

A greatest step forward to the progress. A breakthrough for Irish people, and they deserved it. On May 22th, 2015, Ireland became the 19th country in the world to approve the Gay Marriage, and the first one did it by referendum. An historical event for that country. Congratulations, Irish people!

And Chile... when? At least, we have to be content with the Civil Union Agreement for a while. But we hope someday (sooner) every citizen people, LGBT or not, to be happy. Also, we hope the same for Australian people.
