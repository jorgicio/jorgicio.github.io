Pacquiao v/s Mayweather. Why is it so important?

I know this fight is the most awaited in latter days. But, well... that's just a fight like any other in the world. Also, I bet both Pacquiao any Mayweather are not so known before this event, so... What's the matter? When everybody is interested in boxing since? I respect some people that follow both guys since their careers, but why everybody? That's too fucking mainstream. And that's something I wonder.

I mean, with this, that's not a matter of you can watch it or not, but everybody got me fed up with that people comments every 5 minutes what's happening on TV.

Yes, I got fed up with the TV, specially if it's about showbiz world, but that's something I'll comment in a future post.
